Privacy Policy for Travel Asset Manager

Last Updated: March 2, 2026

Your Data Stays With You: Travel Asset Manager stores all fleet data — trips, vehicles, drivers, and fuel logs — locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any of your data.

1. Information We Collect

Travel Asset Manager stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Fleet Data: Trip records, vehicle details, driver profiles, and fuel log entries you create
  • User Profile: Name and email address you provide during sign-in (stored locally)
  • App Preferences: Backup settings, auto-backup frequency, and backup target preferences

We do NOT collect:

  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Location data
  • Any data transmitted to our servers (we don't operate any)

2. How We Store Your Data

  • Local Database: All fleet data is stored in a SQLite database on your device
  • Backup Files: Local backups are saved as encrypted .tamb files on your device storage
  • No Server Storage: We operate no servers and never receive your data

3. Google Drive Backup (Optional)

If you choose to enable Google Drive backup:

  • What's Backed Up: An encrypted copy of your database is stored in your personal Google Drive account
  • Encryption: Backup files are encrypted before upload and can only be restored within the app
  • Your Control: You can disable or disconnect Google Drive at any time from Settings
  • Google's Role: Google Drive stores your encrypted files under your own Google account. Google's own privacy policy governs their storage service

4. Google Sign-In

When you use Google Sign-In for Drive backup:

  • We only request permission to access files created by Travel Asset Manager in your Drive
  • We receive your Google account email address solely to identify your Drive connection
  • We do not access any other Google services, contacts, or data
  • Your Google credentials are handled entirely by Google's authentication system

5. Data Usage

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Displaying your fleet records, trips, and fuel logs within the app
  • Generating PDF reports for trip records
  • Creating local and/or cloud backups (if enabled)
  • App functionality and user preferences

We do NOT:

  • Share your data with any third parties
  • Use your data for advertising or profiling
  • Sell or monetize your data in any form

6. Data Security

  • Local Storage: All data is stored in an SQLite database on your device, accessible only by the app
  • Encrypted Backups: Backup files use AES-based encryption before being saved locally or uploaded to Drive
  • No Analytics Services: We do not use any third-party tracking, analytics, or crash-reporting services

7. Third-Party Services

Travel Asset Manager uses the following third-party services (only when you enable backup):

  • Google Drive API: For optional encrypted backups (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Google Sign-In: For authentication to access Drive (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)

These services are only activated when you explicitly connect Google Drive in Settings.

8. Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All data is visible and accessible within the app at all times
  • Export: Create encrypted backup files at any time from the Backup screen
  • Delete: Individual records can be deleted; uninstalling the app permanently deletes all local data
  • Disconnect Drive: Disconnect Google Drive at any time from Settings — local data is not affected
  • Portability: Restore data on any device using your backup file

9. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Storage / Files: To save and read local backup files
  • Internet: Only used for Google Sign-In and Google Drive backup when you enable them

10. Children's Privacy

Travel Asset Manager is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Retained until you delete individual records or uninstall the app
  • Drive Backups: Retained in your Google Drive until you manually delete them
  • No Server Storage: We do not operate servers that retain any of your data
