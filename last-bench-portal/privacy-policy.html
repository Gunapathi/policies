Welcome to Last Bench Portal, a video learning platform operated by Netsted Infotech ("we", "us", or "our"), based in Tiruchirappalli, Tamil Nadu, India.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our Android application (Last Bench Portal, package com.lastbenchportal.app).

By using the app, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the application.

2. Who Can Use This App

Last Bench Portal operates on an invite-only basis. Users can only create an account by accepting an invite link issued by an administrator. We do not allow self-registration.

User roles within the platform include:

  • Master Admin / Admin — platform administrators
  • Teacher — educators who upload course content
  • Prime Student — students with access to premium content
  • Student — students with access to standard content

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Authenticating your identity via Google Sign-In
  • Granting role-based access to course content (videos, files)
  • Displaying your name and profile within the platform
  • Matching your Google account to an existing invite issued by an administrator
  • Tracking download counts for uploaded content (used by teachers and admins)
  • Maintaining an audit log of administrative actions for security and accountability
  • Enforcing platform security (account lockout after repeated failed attempts)
  • Enabling offline video playback with encrypted storage on your device

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

5. Offline Storage & On-Device Encryption

Last Bench Portal allows eligible users to download videos for offline viewing. Downloaded content is stored on your device with the following protections:

  • Videos are encrypted using AES-256-CBC encryption before being saved
  • A unique encryption key is generated per user and stored in the Android Keystore (backed by hardware security where available)
  • Encrypted files are stored in a private, per-user directory within the app's sandbox
  • Videos are decrypted only temporarily during playback and cleaned up immediately after
  • Screenshot and screen recording are blocked during video playback using Android's FLAG_SECURE mechanism
If the encryption key is lost (e.g. after app uninstall or device reset), downloaded videos become unreadable by design — you will need to re-download them.

8. Data Security

We implement the following measures to protect your data:

  • All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • Firebase Security Rules enforce strict access controls — users can only access data their role permits
  • Cloudflare R2 content is accessed only through time-limited pre-signed URLs (15 minutes for upload, 1 hour for streaming) — no direct bucket access
  • R2 credentials are stored in Google Secret Manager and never exposed to client devices
  • Role-based access is enforced on both the client (UI) and server (Cloud Functions) — neither can be bypassed independently
  • Rate limiting is applied to authentication attempts: 5 failed attempts within 15 minutes will temporarily lock the account
  • Offline video encryption: AES-256-CBC with Android Keystore-backed keys (described in Section 5)
